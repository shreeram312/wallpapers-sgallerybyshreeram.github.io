What is a Wallpaper..?

What is wallpaper in mobile phone? Wallpaper is a background. It can be a photograph or a drawing etc. It is the background digital image applied to the home screen of a computer or a mobile device. Initially, these devices contain default wallpaper but the user can change them according to their choice.
